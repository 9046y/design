Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) are a key part of the supply chain and provide directed stock rotation,intelligent picking directives, automatic consolidation and cross-docking to maximize the use of valuable warehouse space. Having a WMS in place means you don’t depend any more on people’s experience, the system has the intelligence.

* How can we represent real-world Hierarchical storage structures and support their flexible rearrangement?

* How can we support sequential access to the storage elements of the Hierarchical storage structures without exposing its underlying structure?

* How can we build the Hierarchical storage structures easily?
